# Codebase Platforms

Platforms are reusable capabilities consumed across multiple product or
engineering systems. They own shared contracts and behavior, not a complete
end-user or operator workflow.

- [Product Platforms](product/README.md) — reusable capabilities exposed to or
  consumed by product systems.
- [Engineering Platforms](engineering/README.md) — reusable capabilities for
  engineering systems.
- [Internal Platforms](internal/README.md) — reusable internal capabilities
  that are neither product contracts nor contributor procedures.

Use [Structures](../structures/README.md) for source-area organization and
[Systems](../systems/README.md) for complete product or engineering domains.
